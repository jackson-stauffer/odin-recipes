Cranberry Sauce

Description

This homemade Cranberry Sauce recipe is easy to make with fresh or frozen cranberries, orange juice, and the BEST add-ons like honey, cinnamon, nutmeg, and more!

Ingredients

  • 12 oz. fresh or frozen cranberries, rinsed
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1/2 cup orange juice
  • 1/3 cup sugar
  • 1/4 cup honey
  • 1 teaspoon lemon juice
  • 1 tablespoon cherry juice, optional

Instructions

  1. Add the rinsed cranberries to a medium saucepan.
  2. Add the water, orange juice, sugar, honey, lemon juice, cherry juice (if using), cinnamon, and nutmeg. Stir to combine.
  3. Bring to a boil, then reduce to a simmer. Simmer for 10/12 minutes, the cranberries will have burst and some of the liquid will have reduced.
  4. Remove from heat and let it cool completely. There will still be some liquid, it will continue to thicken upon standing.
  5. Transfer to the fridge and let it cool completely for 2 hours. You can also store it in an airtight container and serve up to 5 days later.
